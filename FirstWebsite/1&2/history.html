Early Years

I was born in a Stockholm and grew up surrounded by family and friends. My childhood was filled with adventure, curiosity, and learning. From an early age,
I was passionate about various activities, including sports and creative projects. I remember spending hours drawing, reading, and exploring nature. These early years shaped my love for discovery and self-expression.


Teenage Years

As a teenager, I experienced a transformative period in my life. This was when I began to define my interests and passions more clearly. I developed a keen interest in history, technology, and the arts. I participated in extracurricular activities such as school plays,
volunteering, and joining clubs that focused on social causes. These years laid the foundation for my future pursuits.

Recent Years

I'm currently studying and focusing on building a strong foundation for my future in tech. Along the way, I've reached several personal milestones that have shaped my journey — from choosing a specialization in interaction design to diving into web development to broaden my skills. My passion for continuous learning and my willingness to take on new challenges have helped me
grow both personally and professionally. These milestones are important steps on my path toward working in the field I love

Significant Events

Birth (1998)

Description: I was born in Stockholm, surrounded by a supportive and loving family. This marked the beginning of my journey of curiosity and growth.
Significance: The start of a life shaped by creativity, learning, and a deep interest in people and technology.

High School Graduation (2017)

Description: After years of hard work and learning, I graduated from high school with a growing interest in tech and design.
Significance: A key step toward further education and the first real spark that led me into the world of user experience and development.

Started University Studies (2018)

Description: I began studying Information and Systems Science with a focus on Interaction Design.
Significance: This program helped me understand how humans interact with digital systems, and confirmed my passion for tech with a human touch.

Diving into Web Development (2024)

Description: I took a dedicated course in web development to broaden my technical skills and bring my design ideas to life.
Significance: This opened the door to frontend development, where I could combine visual creativity with functional code and with this I am currently creating my personal website.
